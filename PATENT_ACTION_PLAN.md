## üÜò TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: USPTO website not working
**Solution:** Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

### Problem: Can't upload large PDF
**Solution:** Compress PDF or split into smaller files

**Solution:** When in doubt, pay Small Entity ($150) - can't hurt

**Solution:** Patent Center will highlight in red - just fill it in

**Solution:** Try different card or contact USPTO help: 1-800-786-9199
