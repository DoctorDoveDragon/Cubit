## üîç USPTO FILING PROCESS

### Step-by-Step Filing:

1. **Create USPTO Account**
   - Go to: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/apply/patent-center
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Verify email

2. **Start Provisional Application**
   - Login to Patent Center
   - Click "Start a New Application"
   - Select "Provisional Application"
   - Application type: "Utility"

3. **Upload Documents**
   - Specification (I'll create this)
   - Drawings (screenshots from your app)
   - Cover sheet (fill online)

4. **Pay Fee**
   - Credit card or USPTO deposit account
   - $75 for micro entity

5. **Submit**
   - Review everything
   - Click submit
   - **Save your confirmation number!**

6. **Receive Confirmation**
   - Email within 24 hours
   - Serial number assigned
   - **You now have "Patent Pending" status!** üéâ

## üí¨ COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I still show my code publicly after filing?**  
A: YES! Once filed, you can share freely.

**Q: What if someone already patented something similar?**  
A: Your specific implementation/mappings are likely unique. Prior art just means you narrow your claims.

**Q: Do I need a prototype?**  
A: You have one! Your working code is the prototype.

**Q: Can I file internationally?**  
A: Yes, but file US provisional first, then PCT within 12 months ($2,000+).

**Q: What if I don't convert to full patent?**  
A: Your provisional becomes public after 12 months, but you keep the documentation.

**Q: Can I update my invention after filing?**  
A: Not in the same application, but you can file continuation applications.
